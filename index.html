Welcome to the Child Language Lab

At the Child Language Lab, we investigate how children acquire and develop language skills. Our research combines playful exploration with rigorous scientific methods.

Did You Know?

Babies can distinguish between different languages even before they start speaking!

Our Research

Our current projects focus on:

  • Early word acquisition processes
  • Bilingual language development
  • Phonological complexity in child speech

"We're particularly interested in how children acquire complex linguistic structures." - Dr. Cournane
